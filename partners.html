Our Valued Partners

We collaborate with leading organizations to expand our reach and maximize our impact across Southern Africa.

University Hospital Basel

A leading Swiss hospital that provides clinical expertise and research support for the PEGISUS program.

South African Medical Research Council

A key partner in South Africa, providing research and development support for our public health interventions.

University of Zambia

Our academic partner in Zambia, helping us to conduct research and implement our program effectively in local communities.

SolidarMed

An international health organization that provides on-the-ground support and implementation expertise for our projects in Zambia and Zimbabwe.

Build It International

Our vocational training partner in Zambia, providing the platform for us to deliver the PEGISUS program to young people.

Masvingo Polytechnic

A key partner in Zimbabwe, hosting our program and providing valuable vocational training to youth in the region.

SOZO Foundation

Our vocational training partner in South Africa, working with us to empower young people in disadvantaged communities.
